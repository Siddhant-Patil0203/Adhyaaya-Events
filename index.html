Technical Events

Avishkar

Explore

Avishkar is the project Based competition to showcase your innovative ideas in the form of projects . Projects can be in the form of working model /software/ simulation based/industry sponsored/experiments. Participants have the liberty of choosing mode of project to be hardware or software as per their convenience. The event would consist of an Eliminator Round where the team will be required to submit the working of the project and post elimination the Questionnaire round where selected participants will get a chance to present their project in front of the judges. Avishkar is the perfect opportunity to bring out the innovator in you!

Caddiction

Explore

CADDiction is Adhyaaya`s competitive 3D designing challenge . The event comprises a reasoning and design based quiz in the first round which will also be the elimination round . MUN

Explore

Model United Nations (MUN) is a simulation of the United Nations where participants play the role of delegates/elected members from different countries/parties and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country/party . We have two divisions under the main event , one being UNHRC i.e. United Nation Human Rights Council and the second one being AIPPM i.e. All India Political Parties Meet.

Vaad Vivaad

Explore

Vaad Vivad is the event that puts the participants' speaking and writing skills to the test . The event comprises of an elocution in the first round where the participants will be required to deliver a speech on the topics given to them beforehand related to social issues/situations followed by an extempore ; a spontaneous speaking round in round 2 and an oxford style debate in round 3 where two participants will be pitted up against other .
